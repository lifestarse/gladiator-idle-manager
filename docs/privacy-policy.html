Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Gladiator Idle Manager ("the App") is developed by LifeStar Studio. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

The App collects minimal data necessary for its functionality:

  • Game Progress: Your game data (gladiator stats, wins, gold, upgrades) is stored locally on your device.
  • Google Play Games Services: If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games, your player name and scores may be shared with Google Play Games leaderboards. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Advertising: The App may display ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • In-App Purchases: Purchase transactions are processed by Google Play. We do not collect or store payment information.

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that collect or store your personal data. Leaderboard data is managed by Google Play Games Services.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Play Games Services (leaderboards)
  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases)

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Deletion

Since all game data is stored locally, you can delete it by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data in your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
